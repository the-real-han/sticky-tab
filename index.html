StickyTab - A always-visible tab on side panel to boost your productivity while multi-tasking

What is StickyTab?

A Chrome Extension that enables the side panel as a tab to simplify multi-tasking.

How to use StickyTab?

StickyTab can be added from Chrome Web Store, then can be opened by right clicking on a link or clicking on the extension icon

Can StickyTab improve your productivity?

Writing your article on StickyTab, while looking at material on other tabs. Talking to ChatGPT on StickyTab, while doing research or actually perfecting your product on another tab. Or just watching youtube videos while working is fine too!
